Trading Bitcoin using emotions can lead to purchasing when greed levels are high. This is typically the best time to sell. Purchasing Bitcoin when the public narative is fearful, is historicalcally the best time to buy. Basing purchassing and selling deecisiosn on historical data can be an effective way to navigate the Bitcoin market. The purpose of this website is to present basic technical indicators which show past trends in Bitcoins price. All of the indicators are simply combinations of Bitcoins price moving averages on a logarithmic scale. Using a logarithmic scale, it can reduce the noise of the tradiational Bitcoin chart and help to visualise trends. There is also a risk metric which is available to premium users. This risk metric can be used to Dollar cost average in and out of the market. For example, if the 'risk' levels are extending beyond neutral, it may be time to start selling portions of your portfolio. If the risk metric dips below the neutral level, it may be a good oportunity to gradually Dollar cost average into the market.
